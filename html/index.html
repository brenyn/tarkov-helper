Welcome to Tarkov Helper

Welcome to Tarkov Helper. The goal of this project is to help you become a better Tarkov player by providing many essential resources in one location.

Read more

Resources

A task flowchart where you can track your progress in the questline by using our checklist, a spreadsheet that keeps track of what items you need to collect depending on where you are in the questline, and a collection of gun builds for you to check out.

  • Quests

    A quest flowchart where you can track your progress in the questline by using our checklist, see what quests come after, watch videos on different quests, and more relevant quest information.

  • Items

    A spreadsheet that tracks what items you need to collect as well as if they need to be found in raid or not. Updates depending on where you are in the questline.

  • Gunsmithing

    A collection of gun builds compiled from our favorite YouTubers/streamers. Includes budget builds up to meta builds for all weapons.

  • Gunsmithing

    A collection of gun builds compiled from our favorite YouTubers/streamers. Includes budget builds up to meta builds for all weapons.

About us

We are a group of passionate Tarkov players who understand that Tarkov can be a rough place at times (most of the time for some of us). Our goal with this project is to make the game more accessible for casual players and more convienent for you hardcore players out there.
